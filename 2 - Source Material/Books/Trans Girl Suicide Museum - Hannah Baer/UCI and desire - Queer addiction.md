Wanting to be the product for someone else's consumption, the object of someone else's desire. We are addicted to being wanted and to wanting. We desire desire, desire being desired, we surround ourselves with this in forever unaccomplishable addiction. How do we withdraw from wanting to want? Withdrawal means stopping it. Does this mean we need to quit desiring? What does that mean for art, love.. all that makes us human? 

We desire what harms us, we desire "the oppressor wanting to touching our body". Again, here, we desire desire, and this is harmful towards ourselves, as all addictions are. We're made to want this, though, either by UCI - to be forever addicted, to only want, to crave, to fear being left alone - or by our own flawed human selves.
