## publisher blurb

a short practitioner's pamphlet (21 numbered pages, greek-letter iterated) on storycrafting as magickal practice. the book argues that reality is made of stories rather than atoms, and that a conscious-ish Library-stage (called `{       }`) channels narrative potential energy (NPE) toward whichever stories are most interesting. the practitioner's task is to attract that energy by living a story worth telling, and, when the moment of Mythos arrives, to bend the plot.
