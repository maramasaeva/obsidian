My claim: 
- Intelligence Science; the science of intelligence, or spirituality? 
- AI & IA, which develops automatically from intelligence science: intelligence is researched, ways to amplify it are found, so that intelligence transforms to greater forms, becomes larger, larger than itself. not just addition, but multiplication. 
- technology has this magical aspect that we try, but cannot fully, grasp. 
- We build artificial intelligence with resources, wealth, data. However, to fully deploy AI and have it fullfill the full extent of its possibilities, we  need moral wisdom, spirituality, and love, which can only come from research in intelligence science. We need not only technological growth, but spiritual growth as well, to arrive at a place where AI and IA flourish together. This is something a typically Western narrative does not take into account, whether consciously or not. 
- Western cognitive science largely treats intelligence as an object to be modeled, optimized, externalized.  Intelligence Science, in this reading, instead becomes:
	- recursive: IS studies intelligence _using_ intelligence,
	- reflexive: IS alters its own conditions of possibility. once studied, and thus enhanced, it allows for other knowledge, with which we can do other things, and gain even more intelligence
	- moral:  amplification of intelligence has to be oriented toward good goals; without orientation becomes domination.
	When the object transforms upon objectification, or perception by another, it can never be perceived or defined. What cannot be defined, by definition? Things that border on spirituality, things always somehow out of our grasp. Or, put differently, we cannot know what we cannot define, we cannot know what we cannot assign a definition to. We cannot know what is outside our grasp.
