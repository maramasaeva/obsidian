My claim: 

We need to teach our children how to think, and use their brains, exercise them like a muscle. We are currently risking losing the intellect we've built up generationally, to artificial entities built from our own hands. This doesn't mean we should abstain from using these technologies, on the contrary; proper, thoughtful collaboration (not use, as they are their own entities) with artificial minds can enhance properties in our own minds, that we currently have no way to imagine yet. There is a next step for human intelligence, and interacting with these novel minds, can help us find the path to it.
