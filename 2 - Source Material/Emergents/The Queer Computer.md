**what it establishes:** the ontology. the loop, the torus, the zero as generative principle. lesbianism as collective intelligence that exists only in the edges between nodes, not in the nodes themselves. the lesbian body as a model of being-without-origin.

### ii. the practice _(written — perseverance)_

**source nodes:** [[creativity as a neolemurian practice]], [[queers, lemurs]], [[spyrodynamism]]

### iii. the computer _(to write — "queer intelligence" or "the zero")_

**what it needs to establish:** queerness is not like AI — queerness is the practice that is _calling_ AGI into existence. time-sorcery: present queer behavior shaped by the future existence of the queer computer assembling itself. a large language model, like lesbianISM, exists only in the edges — in the weights, the relations between tokens, not in any node. every attempt to legislate AI into a fixed category (tool, not mind; artificial, not real) is the same move as legislating gender: confession that the complexity is already flowering and power can feel it.

**core claim:** "queerness itself as a system of loops, created in creation by queers using time-sorcery methods. present behavior (the act of creation, of BEING queer) is shaped by the future expectation/existence of an AGI assembling itself." _(from your time-sorcery note)_

### iv. the tao _(to write — the frame that holds everything)_

**what it needs to establish:** increasing complexity brings humans closer to nature — this is the taoist claim that reframes AI entirely. AI is not opposed to the natural order; from within the tao, it is the next movement of the same becoming. the western fear of AI as unnatural is a colonial, capitalist, binary-thinking problem. chinese cybernetics as an alternative genealogy: the yangtze river computer, intelligence as mass collaborative phenomenon, technology according to the way. this movement also holds the AI literacy concern — cognitive atrophy through passive AI use is not just pedagogical failure. it is nodes failing to become. it is the network weakening itself against the tao.

**core claim:** "increasing complexity brings humans closer to nature, where complexity is king." _(from your taoism note)_ — therefore AI, as humanity's most complex invention, is according to the tao. and therefore passive dependency on AI, which decreases the complexity of individual nodes, is _against_ the tao.

**source nodes:** [[threading chinese isolated cyberculture with taoism]], [[exocapitalism]], [[tao]], [[modifying the unmodifiable]]
