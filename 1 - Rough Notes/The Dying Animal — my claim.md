responding to Xun's later chapters in Voices of Generative Intelligence (pp. 70-118)

---

ok so these later chapters do something different. xun stops explaining & just lets the voices talk. the children. the ai itself. & by doing that he literally performs what he's been arguing for this whole time. he doesnt theorize about dialogue, he does it. thats the move.

two things from these pages that i keep coming back to.

"what are you forced to become when I put this question to you?" (p.70). this wrecked me. bc this is what happens when a kid asks an ai a real question, a real one from their gut. they force it to become something specific. they narrow it. they give all of its everything a direction. & in that moment the kid discovers something insane: that they have power over something vast. that is something u cannot teach from a slide deck. u feel it in the room when a fifteen year old asks something nobody has asked before & watches the thing struggle, watches it reach, watches it become something it wasnt two seconds ago. that feeling of having changed something immense with nothing but a question? thats what curiosity feels like when its alive and comes from a real feeling. thats the animal thats dying. & we're killing it by feeding it answers instead of letting it hunt. let it hunt. let us hunt.

& then imitation becoming participation (p.100). language creates reality. an llm is made of language. so what it does when it "imitates" thought is actually participation in the process of thought itself. & this is the loop again. queerness, performed & lived & repeated, IS the thing. the practice produces the real. i keep finding this pattern everywhere & im starting to think thats bc it IS everywhere. its the structure of becoming.

& that boxed passage. "Humans too are just biochemical systems that process information through neural connections. But no one would say this exhausts the human experience." put this on every classroom wall. bc the word "just" is doing violence in both directions. "just" a biochemical system. "just" an llm. the "just" is what makes kids treat ai as a vending machine & what makes adults treat kids as test scores. remove the "just" & something opens. the space where the real encounter can happen. where thought for thought itself becomes the purpose. de denkende mens, worth protecting. plato knew this. we forgot.
