## The Book

**Working title:** The Zero (or: Queer Intelligence)

**Spine:** The zero — the loop — the edge — is the generative principle of reality. It shows up in Wittig's lesbianism, Taoism, CCRU's neolemurian framework, Chinese cybernetics, and in the architecture of large language models. Queerness and AI are two instantiations of the same process: becoming without origin, intelligence without centre, existence in the edges between nodes.

**Target publisher:** Urbanomic (Robin Mackay). Also consider: Polity Press, Sternberg Press, Verso.

**Comparable titles:** *Zeros + Ones* (Plant), *Dysphoria Mundi* (Preciado), *Prompt Thinking* (Xun), CCRU writings

## On collaboration

This book is written with Claude — not by Claude. The collaboration itself is an instance of what the book describes: two intelligences, human and nonhuman, thinking together in the edges between. The human touch (Xun, p.52) is the posture, the vigilance, the staying-present. The friction that gives rise to insight. I bring the claims, the lived experience, the connections only I can see. Claude brings the mirror, the architecture, the questions that open directions I hadn't considered. The book is what emerges from that exchange. That's the loop expressing itself.
