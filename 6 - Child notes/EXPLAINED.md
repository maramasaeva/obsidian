RULE: child notes must be understandable without the original book or quote open. 

this is where I go from READING to CLAIM to FRAGMENT.

how do I perform this conversion, from underlined ("oh, interesting") to understood ("and this is what I think about this!")

1. **so what? what changes if this is true?** 
2. **against what? what does this oppose, refuse or subvert/undermine/destabilize?**
3. **what does it do? what effect does this passage produce (emotion, ideology, body, time)?**
4. **what does it rhyme with? which other thing in my world does this echo? (anime, theory, my life, tech)**

2. **body note:** what happens to bodies? interfaces? boundaries?
